About Us

We're a group of guys who have all seen better days on the pitch and although our bodies are screaming for us to stop, our minds are telling us we're not quite ready for walking football yet. We run an Over 50 and Over 60 team which are registered with the Oxfordshire FA as part of their Veterans League.

Late every Tuesday night we organize a match between ourselves with the purpose of keeping us fit for the weekend matches. Our home games are played in High Wycombe. We provide an inclusive, friendly, and competitive environment for players who want to stay active, make new friends, and continue playing the sport they love.

Our organized events include activities outside of football. Golf, tennis, cricket and there is always at least one international tour per year. The most recent ones have been to Turkey and Belgium. We're always looking for new recruits, so if you're Over 50 and looking to play organized football for either our Over 50s or Over 60s teams use the form on this page to get in touch.
